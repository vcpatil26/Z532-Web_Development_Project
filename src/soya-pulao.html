Soya Pulao

Soyabean Chunks

Basmati (type of rice)

Ingredients
Item Quantity
Basmati Rice 2 cups
Soya Chunks 1 Cup
Sliced Onion 1 tbsp
Egg 1/2 cup
Garlic Flakes 15
Sliced Ginger 1
Tomato 1
Garam Masala Powder 1 tbsp
Red Chilli Powder 2 tbsp
Oil 2 tbsp
Chopped Coriander Leaves To taste
Salt To taste

Recipe
  1. Wash rice with water and allow it to drain completely.
  2. Put Soya chunks in boiling water and stir it for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from flame and keep it aside for at least 1 hour so that soya becomes soft.
  4. After an hour wash soya chunks with cold water. Squeeze them to remove any traces of water.
  5. Heat 1 Tablespoon of oil in a pressure pan. Put bay leaf .and sliced onions
  6. Add finely cut garlic and ginger. Stir till golden brown.
  7. Add soya chunks. Put chilli powder and garam masala with salt.
  8. Mix thoroughly, add rice and tomato (already cut into 4 pieces). Stir well.
  9. Add 3 and half cups of hot water to the mixture, close the pressure pan.
  10. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes, on medium flame.
  11. Remove from flame. Allow to cool it. Serve with garnished coriander leaves
